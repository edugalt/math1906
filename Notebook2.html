2.4 Stability Analysis¶

<font color=red> Stability analysis </red>¶

Fixed point: $$ \lambda = 2- a \Rightarrow |\lambda|< 1 \text{ for } 1< a <3$$ Periodic orbit of period 2: $$ \lambda_2 = -a^2+2a+4 \Rightarrow |\lambda| < 1 \text{ for } 3< a < 1+\sqrt{24}/2 \approx 3.45$$ </font>
